The Return Trip

The files are converted into multiple TCP data, each containing the IP address of your ISP and your computer. These pass through the server's firewall.

On to the Internet, they are routed by the routers, back to the ISP, through your connection and into your computer where they are reconstructed into temporary files on your drive.

Your browser then uses the XHTML markup file to display the text as well as any other files such as graphics or media files.

Financial Aid Program Requirements

Financial Assistance Available

To be eligible for this program, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Have three children or more children under the age of twelve.
  • Have an income below the federal poverty line.
  • Be gainfully employed.
  • Not have any legal convictions.

To apply, please do the following:

  1. Pick up an application for MoneyPlus™.
  2. Assemble the following papers.
    1. Your completed application
    2. Doctor's record of your child's diagnosis.
    3. Tax records or a certificate of your income level.
    4. A letter from your employer.
  3. Make an appointment to meet with a MoneyPlus™ volunteer.

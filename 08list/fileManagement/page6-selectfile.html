You will find that selecting files from the keyboard can be a lot quicker and safer then if you use the mouse.

Here's how!

To select a group of files:

This video shows how this works.
  • Hold down the SHIFT key

  • Click on the first item of the list (keep holding down that SHIFT key!

  • Click on the last item of the list

To select individual files or toggle a selection on/off:
  • Hold down the CTRL key
    On the Mac use the Command key

  • Click on the item you want to select or deselect (keep holding down that CTRL key!

Your try it.

On your computer or flash drive make a new set of folders and sub-folders.

Here are the steps you can follow:

  1. Open up Windows Explorer (or Finder on the Mac)
     
  2. Navigate to the place where you want to create your new file structure.
     
  3. Make new folders and sub-folders to match the graphic on the right.

Important!

When creating folders and subfolders keep your structure as simple as possible.

It gets very tedious clicking through a series of subfolders to locate a file.

The extra subfolders used in this tutorial are to help you see how the path looks.

Most often all you need is a root directory and a graphic folder.

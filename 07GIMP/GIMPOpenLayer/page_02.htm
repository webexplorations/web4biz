Overview of GIMP Interface

When you first start up GIMP (GNU Image Manipulation Program) the standard tool box will display in the upper left corner of your screen.

You can change the size and position of this toolbox area any time you want. Just click and drag an edge or click and drag the title bar and move the window where you want it.

If the Toolbox isn't displaying use CTRL B or choose Windows/Toolbox from the menu bar.

There are a lot of options here, but don't let that worry you. Most of them will just sit there and wait for you until you want to use them.

As you click on an item, the options will change in the bottom half of the window.

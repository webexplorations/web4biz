Web Development - SEO

Include Pertinent Links

Include links that relate to what your customer will want.

More information? More details? Other alternatives? Think about the links that add value to the pages you look at out on the Web.

Don't use the non-descript "click here". Instead write copy that describes the link and make that copy the link. Which link would you use?

Click Here vs. How to write effective links

Link to the other pages in your site

Include lots of links to other pages on your site, not just in the menus. This will give you a better link ratio. For example, if your home page talks about the history of your Gizmo(r) product, link to the product page showing the current model. This will make your link profile more trustworthy and ensure that the spider indexes all of the pages in your site.

Don't link to insignificant pages

Links to your privacy policy and shipping rates on every page will lower your link value. Each time a spider comes to a repeating link it thinks that link is more important. You can exclude these pages from redundant searching by using a robot.txt file. (See below.)

Web Development - SEO

Limit Flash navigation

Flash may look good but...

I know, you've seen these really nice web sites that use Flash introductions. But, there's a price to pay here. If you are really serious about SEO, don't make Flash the focus of your site. Search engines will not index the content inside a Flash object. Sure, the designer can put in a few search strings, but you can't be certain that is being done and the search engines are not written to analyze Flash objects, they are written to analyze pure text.

Flash is best used for "movie" demonstrations. All the information that is given by the Flash object should also be represented in text form somewhere else on the page.

You may have the best looking pages in the world, but if you aren't showing up on the search engines then you won't have a lot of people coming to your site.

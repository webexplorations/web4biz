Use a unique title and description

Use a pertinent title and a succinct description.

Probably one of the most important parts of every page is the <title> element and the description meta tag.

The search spiders use these two page elements to determine the keywords for the page. The words used in the title should also be in the description as well as throughout the content of the page.

Here's an example: Imagine creating a page that talks about the 60th anniversary of a company. The page has historical photos and shows the user how much the company has changed, especially highlighting its newest product, the Gizmo.

But, if the title is "A Big Day" and the description is "A great time was had by all at our celebration" the search engines won't rank the site near as high as if you would have used "60th Anniversary Celebration Highlights Growth of theGizmo" and the description read "The 60th Anniversary Celebration highlights the historical growth of our new product line from simple wire based communications to the newest addition, The Gizmo"

I've highlighted the keywords so you can imagine what is in the contents of this page.

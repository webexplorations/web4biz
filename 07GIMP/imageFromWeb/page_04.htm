Using Photos from the Web

Downloading the Image

When you find the photo you would like to use click on the thumbnail to get to the photo page.

Right-mouse click on the image and you will see size choices. Pick the largest one available. It is okay to take a large photo and reduce it, but you'll get a fuzzy image if you take a small image and try to make it larger.

Once you click on a size you'll get a download page. Make note of the photographer's name because that is one thing you need to include if you use his or her photo.

Save the file in a special directory using a short descriptive name, the file size, and the photographer's name. (See the next page for examples).

If you click on the Additional Information - Some rights reserved link you will see what the photographer expects if you use his or her photo. Most often all you have to do is give credit (attribution) of who shot the photo.

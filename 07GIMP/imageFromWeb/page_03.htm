Using Photos from the Web

Use Advanced Search

In order to see the available photos on Flickr.com use the standard search field at the top of the home page. Type in your search term. When the results show up you will see a link to "Advanced Search".

Click on the magnifying glass to do a search.

Look for a link (near the search box) that says "Advanced Search". Click on that link.

Scroll down until you find an option for Creative Commons. Check the boxes that cover the type of creative commons you need and then click on the Search button.

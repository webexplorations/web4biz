Getting Photos from the Web

IMPORTANT! Software out on the cloud is constantly changing, sometimes on a weekly basis. The screens may look different in the current version of Flickr.com. However, the basic tools such as Advanced Search, Creative Commons selections, and saving images will be the same. You just have to adapt to how these tools are currently being presented on the site.

Just because images are on the Web does not mean you have permission to use them.

Many individuals are willing to share their photos for free. Normally the only requirement is that you include a photo credit showing who took the photo.

This tutorial will show you how to get free photos from the Web using the Flickr.com website.

You try it.

Use the files in the uncompressed folder named dogCat and try all three methods to select and unselect different groups of files.

Here are the three methods:

  1. Click on the first item,
    Hold down the SHIFT key,
    Click on the last item in the list.
     
  2. Hold down the CTRL key,
    Click on an item to select/unselect it.
     
  3. Click and drag the mouse to "touch" a group of files. Every file that is "touched" will be selected.

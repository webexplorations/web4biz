7. Choose the correct word. Use our contact form and __ sales rep will be in touch with you.
*a. a
b. an
@Always use an before a word that starts with a consonant. Use a when the next word starts with a vowel or a vowel sound (union, uniform, sounds like a "Y")

8. Choose the correct word. Each of our web pages ____ handmade and designed for your company.
*a. is
b. are
@Each is singular. There is only one so "is" is correct. Don't let the "web pages" part fool you!

9. Which sentence is correct? your or you're
a. Smile, your saving a lot of money.
*b. Smile, you're saving a lot of money.
@In this example you're is a contraction for "you are". Your is a word designating that something belongs to you (a possessive).

10. Which sentence is correct? your or you're
*a. Your satisfaction is our number one priority.
b. You're satisfaction is our number one priority.
@Your shows possesion. The satisfaction belongs to you. If you try to put the words "you are" in place of "your" it doesn't make sense.

11. Which sentence is correct? its or it's - Family Dining
a. Family dining at it's best!
*b. Family dining at its best!
@It's is a contraction for "it is". Its is possessive describing the family dining. If you try to say "Family dining at it is best!" it doesn't make sense. So, no apostrophe.

@Lose is when you have lost something. Loose is the opposite of "tight". Loose is like He had a nut loose.

Computer Careers Web Hosting

Summary

All web programming students at South Central College are given a web hosting account that they can use as part of their studies. This information sheet will show you how to use this resource to publish your web pages.

Our web hosting features CPanel, a popular interface used by many web hosting companies on the Web. cPanel gives you the ability to set up and mySQL databases, set passwords, and FTP to your site.

Login Information

 

To view your website: http://firstnameLastinitial.t.southcentral.edu

To access your cPanel: https://firstnameLastinitial.t.southcentral.edu:2083 (note the https - this uses a secure http connection)

Your user name:   The first 8 letters (or less if your name is shorter) of your firstnameLastinitial  (Using your name as listed in the MnSCU class rosters and your DARS report)

Your password:  Your student id including leading zeros
